# Drop your assets here

## folders

- `characters/` — character PNGs or SVGs (transparent background works best)
- `backgrounds/` — scene backgrounds (1920×1080 or similar 16:9)
- `props/` — objects, effects, overlays
- `audio/` — music and sound effects (coming soon)

## naming

Use clear names: `kitten-happy.png`, `garden-day.jpg`, etc.

When you add images, tell me the filenames and I'll wire them into the cartoon scenes.
